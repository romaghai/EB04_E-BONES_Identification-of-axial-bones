VERTEBRAL COLUMN

DETAILS ON VERTEBRAL COLUMN


1. Cervical vertebrae: They are seven in number & present in neck and forms cervical curve
Ist cervical vertebra is called Atlas that forms joint with occipital bone.

Second cervical vertebra is Axis. It forms joint with atlas & helps turn head from side to side (saying No).

2. Thoracic vertebrae : articulates with ribs and forms thoracic curve.

3. Lumbar vertebrae: forms lower back and forms lumbar surve.


Functions of vertebral column

1. Protects Spinal cord.

2. Supports skull.

3. Provides structural support and balance to the body.

4. Maintains posture, provides 2/5 th height of the human body.

5. Intervertebral discs have shock absorbing function and give flexibility to vertebral column.

RIBS (12 pairs)
  1. 1st rib does not move during respiration
  2. Spaces between ribs contain intercostal muscles that help in respiration
  3. First 7 pairs are attached to sternum by costal cartilage (TRUE RIBS)
  4. 8th , 9th and 10th are not directly attached to sternum (FALSE RIBS)
  5. 11th and 12th are the floating ribs (no anterior attachment)
